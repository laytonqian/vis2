Renewable Energy Consumption
This visualisation is is about Renewable Energy Consumption from the 1965-2018. Renewable Energy can be collected from many different sources and the main sources which this visualisation focuses on are hydropower, solar power and wind power.

Hydropower is a very mature technology that has been growing steadily for years. While on the other hand, solar and wind power are quite new, experiencing rapid growth since the start of the 21st century.

First world European countries consume the most renewable energy when taking population into account whereas overall, the countries with larger gdps consume the most renewable energy.
